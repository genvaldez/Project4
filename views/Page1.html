Binary numbers are important because using them instead of the decimal system, helps simplifing the design of computers and other techologies.

In order to translate between decimal numbers and binary numbers we will look at the top table. We can see that 0 and 1 are the same in either stystem, starting at 2 things start to change. The decimal 2 looks like a 10 in binary, but we start from the right and number each space by powers of 2, so the first position is at 20, second position is at 21, 3rd position is at 22,etc. Note that it stops depending on how many spaces you have open. In this case we have 4 open spots, yet we only calculate the space where we would get a result of 2 and that is the second spot as shown in the graph.
